Platespot

Platespot is an app designed to enhance the security and management of private estates by efficiently tracking vehicles in the premises. Utilizing Google Sheets as a robust backend database, Platespot offers a comprehensive solution for estate security personnel to monitor and manage vehicle access.

About

Integrating OCR technology with Google Sheets, it simplifies vehicle management and enhances estate security with real-time data and daily reports.

Effortless Vehicle Scanning

Utilize OCR technology to quickly scan and categorize vehicles. This feature ensures a streamlined process for identifying each vehicle's status, enhancing the efficiency of security operations.

Dynamic Data Management

Experience the ease of automated data handling with Google Sheets integration. The system not only records vehicle data but also organizes it efficiently, allowing for easy access and management by the security team.

Analytics Dashboard

Stay informed with our intuitive dashboard that displays daily vehicle statistics. This visual tool aids in tracking the flow of registered, unregistered, and visitor vehicles, providing valuable insights for estate management.

Features

Designed to enhance estate security, our app is equipped with advanced features that simplify vehicle management.

Instant License Plate Recognition

Utilize OCR technology for quick and accurate scanning of vehicle license plates.

Automated Vehicle Categorization

The app instantly categorizes vehicles into registered, unregistered, or visitor status, streamlining the monitoring process.

Real-Time Data Synchronization

Seamlessly integrates with Google Sheets for real-time data updating and retrieval.

Daily Traffic Analysis

Generates daily graphical reports, offering clear insights into the volume and types of vehicles within the estate.

Secure User Authentication

Employs Google OAuth 2.0 for robust and secure user login, ensuring data protection and privacy.

User-Friendly Interface

Designed for ease of use, allowing security personnel to efficiently operate the app with minimal training.
